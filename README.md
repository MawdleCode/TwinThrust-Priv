# Privacy Policy — Twin Thrust

**Last updated: 12 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game **Twin Thrust** ("the game",
"we", "us") handles information. By installing or playing the game you agree to
this policy.

## Summary

The game itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
about you, and it does not require an account or login. The only data leaving
your device comes from two third-party Google services the game uses: **Google
AdMob** (to show ads) and **Google Play Billing** (to process optional in-app
purchases). Those services are described below.

## Information the game stores on your device

The game saves your progress and settings (such as completed levels, chosen
rocket, and audio options) **locally on your device**. If you have enabled
Android backup, your device's operating system may include this file in your
Google account backup so your progress can be restored on reinstall. We never
receive or have access to this data ourselves.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)

The game shows optional **rewarded video ads** (which you choose to watch to
unlock content) through **Google AdMob**, a Google advertising service. To serve
ads, AdMob may collect and process information such as your device's advertising
identifier, device and network information, and approximate (non-precise)
location. We do not control and do not receive this data; it is handled by
Google under its own policies.

For information on how Google uses this data, see:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

### Consent
Where required (for example in the UK and the European Economic Area), the game
presents a **consent request** managed by Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP)
before personalised ads are shown, so you can choose how your data is used.

### Children
The game is suitable for, and may appeal to, children. Ads in the game are
served in a **child-directed / "designed for families"** mode: they are
**non-personalised** and are restricted to age-appropriate content in line with
Google AdMob's families and child-directed policies. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children for advertising or any other purpose.

## In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

The game offers optional one-time purchases (to unlock levels and cosmetic
content) processed entirely through **Google Play Billing**. Payment is handled
by Google; we never see or store your payment details. Google provides us only
with confirmation of which purchases an account owns, so the game can unlock the
content you bought. See the Google Play Terms of Service and Google's Privacy
Policy (above) for how Google handles purchase data.

## Data we receive

We, the developer, do **not** collect, receive, sell, or share any personal data
about you. We have no servers, no analytics, and no accounts.

## Your choices

- You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your
  device's Android settings.
- You can change or withdraw ad consent at any time (where the consent form is
  offered) via the in-game settings or by reinstalling.
- You can clear locally stored progress by uninstalling the game.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
by the "Last updated" date above.
